Detailed Description

The Process Segment Definition Configuration.

In most manufacturing process like (sanding, painting, etc.) it includes a series of process paths (strokes). Each of the process paths may have an approach, departure and transition step.

Example: In the case of painting, when approaching the part you may want to take a specific trajectory and the same for when leaving the part.
